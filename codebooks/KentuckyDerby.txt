Kentucky Derby Results
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Description
The Kentucky Derby data contains the results of the Kentucky Derby, a 1.25-mile horse race held annually at the Churchill Downs racetrack in
Louisville, Kentucky for the years 1896-2017 (Wikipedia contributors 2018).

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Format
This data frame contains the following columns:

year
year of the race

winner
the winning horse

condition
the track condition which has been grouped into three categories: fast, good (which includes the official designations “good” and “dusty”), and slow (which includes the designations “slow”, “heavy”, “muddy”, and “sloppy”).

speed
the average speed (in feet per second) of the winner

starters
number of starters (horses who raced)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Source
Wikipedia contributors. 2018. “Kentucky Derby.” In Wikipedia. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kentucky_Derby.
